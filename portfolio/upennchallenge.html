UPenn Design Challenge

Problem Statement

Eldery patients commonly suffer from Postoperative Cognitive Decline (POCD) or Delirium, a threatening cognitive issue, after waking up from post-surgery anesthesia. This disorder can excite hysteria and lead to long-term cognitive decline for these eldery patients if not properly managed and mitigated. Develop a solution to improve the current state of POCD management and aid its respective stakeholders.
