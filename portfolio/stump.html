Designer's Block

Connected Gifts Technology - Carnegie Mellon University | Fall 2016

Scope

Develop a product or system which integrates the 'Connected Gifts' technology developed by Dr. Jon Cagan and Dr. Daragh Bryne.

Understanding the Technology

The 'Connected Gifts' technology enables two seperated persons to give their loved ones a gift at any moment to brighten their loved one's day. The technology integrates a locked gift box with an app.

Brainstorming

Equipped with the IP a number of areas for product development were brainstormed. Toys were chosen as the area with the most potentional for commercializing and therefore pursued.
