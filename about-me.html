Hello! I'm Raha, a Human-Centered Design Researcher and Strategist!

I explore, analyze and understand how people experience products, services, and environments in order to develop insights on how to better design these experiences for real people.

I thrive in environments where I can be agile and have room to explore. I always aim to constantly learn from experiences, literature, and peers, while sharing my experience and expertise as well.

I develop solutions that keep users at the heart of the process, regardless of the medium the solution takes form in. I believe in championing good ideas, no matter their origin, advocating when passionate about a feature or insight, yet being flexible and pivoting when the insights lead to a new direction.
